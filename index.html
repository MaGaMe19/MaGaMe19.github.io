Who perfectionis is

Perfectionis GmbH has been founded by Dr. Vincent Metzler, an established executive in the international pharmaceutical & biotechnology industry.

Overall, Vincent brings over 20 years of commercial operations experience in the international top-pharma and biotech industry, covering the areas of orphan/ultra-orphan drugs, cell therapies, targeted therapies and supportive care, in various therapeutic areas such as oncology, hematology, nephrology, hepatology, neurology and immunology.

Vincent holds a Ph.D. in Molecular Biology from the University of Berne, Switzerland. He speaks fluently English, French and German.

Besides leading Perfectionis GmbH, Vincent also is the CEO of Kither Biotech, a preclinical stage biopharmaceutical company developing novel therapies for rare pulmonary diseases with high unmet medical needs. The company’s expertise comprises the effective delivery of signal transduction inhibition to the respiratory system, offering a strong potential for improved efficacy and safety in the lung for patients with Cystic Fibrosis, Idiopathic Pulmonary Fibrosis and other respiratory diseases.

Before founding Perfectionis GmbH, Vincent acted as Vice President, Head of Marketing and Commercial Operations, Europe, at Atara Biotherapeutics, a leading immunotherapy company developing allogeneic, off-the-shelf virus-specific T cells for the treatment of virus-associated disorders like Epstein-Barr Virus-positive Post-Transplant Lymphoproliferative Disease (EBV+ PTLD). In this role Vincent was member of the European Leadership Team, driving the commercial expansion of Atara Biotherapeutics’ operations across the major European markets, and leading all marketing and commercial operations strategies and initiatives for the launch of the first cell therapy of its kind in Europe, in an ultra-rare disease.

Previously, Vincent served as a member of the Executive Leadership Team of Wilson Therapeutics, a Swedish biotech company in clinical-stage development of a novel therapeutic agent for Wilson Disease, a rare disease of impaired copper metabolism. He played a pivotal role in the M&A activities that led to the acquisition of Wilson Therapeutics by Alexion in May 2018, through a public cash offer corresponding to a total company value of approx. SEK 7.1 billion (USD 855 million).

Earlier in his career, Vincent was holding senior commercial leadership positions at Alexion Pharmaceuticals, lastly as Head of EMEA Marketing Soliris® & Core Member of the Soliris® Global Leadership Team, and held various marketing & sales positions at Miltenyi Biotec GmbH, F. Hoffmann-La Roche Ltd, Amgen Inc. and Idexx Laboratories. During his commercial career, Vincent did lead several key local and international pharmaceutical product launches.
